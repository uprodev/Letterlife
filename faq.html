We develop Letterlife together with patients, doctors and scientist. If you have any questions you can reach us at hello@letterllife.se. If you have questions related to treatment or ADHD support see in app for access to experts and tools.

    We help everyone and never discriminate! We focus on women because there is a lack of research and evidence-based tools that are developed with women’s specific needs such as hormonal cycles in mind.
