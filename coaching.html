What is ADHD Coaching?

ADHD coaching is like having a personal guide who understands how your brain works and helps you navigate the challenges that come with ADHD. It's not about changing who you are but discovering your strengths and finding ways to manage things like:

  • 25, 45 or 60 min sessions
  • Staying organized & focused
  • Dealing with emotions
  • Someone that kicks your ass

Your coach will work with you to set goals, break them into smaller tasks, and support you in reaching them. It's a positive and personalized approach to help you succeed in school, relationships, and other aspects of life, making things a bit easier and more fun along the way.

What to expect from a LetterLife Coach

  1. Set a plan based on validated methods

    Our coaches tailor a plan based on your ADHD-Type and your current needs. Letterlife coaches work with evidence-based methods like Motivational Interviewing, Community Reinforcement Approach, or CBT-practices.

  2. Your needs guides the process

    Your responsibility is to together with your coach define your needs, reflect on your challenges, and envision your goals. With LetterLife, your self-awareness is the compass guiding us towards the outcomes you desire.

  3. Challenge your coach

    Challenge your coach, and ask specific questions. Probe into their approach to habit and behavioral change. LetterLife's coaches are committed to understanding your unique journey.

  4. Long term change is the goal

    Prioritize long-term benefits, our experienced coaches deliver meaningful change in fewer sessions, drawing upon years of expertise. Together you tailor pace and frequency after your ADHD-type and needs.

  5. A trusted ally and companion

    A strong connection with your coach is fundamental. At LetterLife, we emphasize personalities that click, communication that feels effortless, trust that grows, and a sense of collaboration that propels you towards success.
